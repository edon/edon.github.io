Edon Kelmendi

Max Planck Institute for Software Systems

I am a research associate at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems working with Joël Ouaknine. Before this, I was working with Mikołaj Bojańczyk in Warsaw.

I did my PhD under the supervision of Hugo Gimbert and François Dufour in LaBRI, Bordeaux.

My interests are: games, automata, logics in computer science
